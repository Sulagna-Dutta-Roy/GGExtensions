Pong Game

Welcome to the Pong Game! Here are the instructions:

  • Player 1 (Left Paddle): Use 'W' and 'S' keys to move up and down.
  • Player 2 (Right Paddle): Use the 'Up' and 'Down' arrow keys to move up and down.
  • Hit the ball with your paddle to send it back to the opponent.
  • The game ends when a player reaches 5 points.

Congratulations!
